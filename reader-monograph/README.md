## 1. What this directory is

This directory is the **working scaffold** for an extension of [`gnn.md`](../gnn.md) that develops the **information-theoretic and probabilistic toolkit** used by Paper 2 ([`../PAPER2-SCOPE.md`](../PAPER2-SCOPE.md)) and the surrounding literature (Feder–Merhav, Han–Verdú, Hellman–Raviv, Hashlamoun–Varshney–Samarasooriya, Massey, Cover–Thomas, Devroye–Györfi–Lugosi, Tishby, Breiman, Xu et al., Morris et al.), and folds in [`../PAPER-ARXIV.md`](../PAPER-ARXIV.md) §3.2 + Appendix A as adjusted theory.

The **prose chapters live in `../gnn.md`** as Chapters 9–16 appended after the existing eight chapters. **This directory holds only**:

- [`PLAN.md`](PLAN.md) — the multi-phase delivery plan with per-chapter construct ledger.
- [`references.bib`](references.bib) — canonical bibliography for all new chapters.
- [`requirements.txt`](requirements.txt) — Python deps for the notebooks.
- [`notebooks/`](notebooks/) — 13 Jupyter notebooks that back the new chapters.
- [`plots/`](plots/) — figures generated by the notebooks and `![]()`-linked from `gnn.md`.
- [`exercises/`](exercises/) — per-chapter exercise stubs; solutions land in `solutions/`.
- [`solutions/`](solutions/) — worked solutions appendix (Phase 7).

Where `gnn.md` Ch 1–8 dig into the **partition / WL-refinement** side of expressivity, the new Ch 9–16 stay on the **probability + entropy + Bayes-risk** side — the constructs the cited authors actually use to bound error in terms of information.

## 2. What this reader is NOT

- Not a measure-theory textbook. We stay "measure-free" in the operational sense: a sub-σ-algebra is *the set of questions answered by a statistic `T(X)`*. No Carathéodory, no Lebesgue, no monotone-class arguments.
- Not a partition-combinatorics monograph. Partition refinement is touched only as the operational consequence of *conditioning on a finer statistic*. The combinatorial / WL side lives in [gnn.md](../gnn.md).
- Not a research paper. Conjectures and verdicts from Paper 2 enter only in Chapter 14, framed as *consumers* of the toolkit.

## 3. Reading paths

- **Self-study, full course.** Chapters 0 → 15 in order.
- **Refresher for Paper 2 readers.** Ch 2 → Ch 4 → Ch 5 → Ch 8 → Ch 14.
- **Tight-bound aficionados.** Ch 5 → Ch 6 → Ch 7 → Ch 10.
- **Algorithmic / verified-arithmetic angle.** Ch 5 → Ch 9 → Ch 13.
