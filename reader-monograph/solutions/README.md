## Why solutions are inline

The monograph follows a *roadmap → defs/theorems with proofs → worked
example → exercises with full solutions* contract for every chapter
(see `../PLAN.md`). Splitting solutions into a separate appendix would
break the pedagogical flow and force readers to context-switch between
files. The inline format trades file length for reading continuity,
which is the right trade for a learning monograph.
